This is Yehonatan and I's Thunkable Project called Vegetable Shinobi. In this game you play as a powerful ninja and must save vegetables from hot lava. You play the game by tapping on the vegetables as they fall to the ground, if they touch the ground/lava you lose a life. The player has a total of 5 lives.
